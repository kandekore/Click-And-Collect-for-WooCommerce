Powerful Features to Enhance your WooCommerce Store

Collection Day Settings

Define the opening and closing times for collections for each day of the week. This ensures accurate scheduling of collection times based on your business's operational hours.

Collection Time Booking Window

This feature allows you to set a minimum number of hours required for advanced bookings, preventing customers from selecting collection times that are too close to the current time.

Shipping Methods Settings

Select the shipping methods that support click and collect. This ensures that only relevant shipping methods are available for customers to choose collection dates and times during the checkout process.

Future Collections Widget

Stay on top of your game with our custom dashboard widget. View upcoming collection orders based on the defined collection date and time to streamline your order fulfillment process.

Easy Installation & Management

Click & Collect for WooCommerce is straightforward to install and requires WooCommerce to operate. Once active, you can easily manage its settings from your WordPress dashboard, adjusting the Booking Window, Collection Time, and Shipping Methods to suit your business requirements.
