Why Choose Click & Collect for WooCommerce?

Seamless Integration with WooCommerce: Click & Collect for WooCommerce seamlessly integrates with the powerful WooCommerce platform, leveraging its extensive features and functionalities.

Flexible Collection Options: Customize the collection settings to match your specific business requirements. Provide your customers with the flexibility to choose their preferred collection dates and times.

Enhanced Functionality with Branch Management: Take your click and collect experience to new heights with the complementary plugin, Simple Click & Collect Branches for WooCommerce. Easily manage multiple pickup locations or branches.

Advanced Booking Window: Set a minimum number of hours required for advanced booking to ensure efficient order management.

Tailored Collection Time Settings: Customize the opening and closing times for collection on each day of the week to align with your business hours.

Selective Shipping Methods: Choose specific shipping methods that support click and collect and offer a curated selection of shipping options to your customers.
